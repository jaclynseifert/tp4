Triple Chocolate Chunk Cookies

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • teaspoon vanilla extract>
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 large egg
  • ¾ teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup milk chocolate chips
  • 1 (3 ounce) bar dark chocolate, cut into chunks
  • 3 tablespoons white chocolate chips

Kitchen Equipment

  • Oven
  • 13" x 9" pan
  • Measuring cups
  • Mixing Bowl

Directions

  1. Combine brown sugar, butter, white sugar, and salt in a large bowl; beat with an electric mixer until a creamy, deep brown mixture forms.
  2. Add egg and vanilla; beat until mixture lightens and becomes smooth, 10 to 15 seconds.
  3. Mix flour and baking soda together in a separate bowl. Add slowly to the wet ingredients until mostly incorporated, but some white traces of flour remain. Fold in milk chocolate, dark chocolate, and white chocolate using a spatula, not the mixer. Cover the dough and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, or up to 48 hours.
  4. Spread graham cracker crumbs evenly over bottom of pan.
  5. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) when ready to bake.
  6. Divide cold dough into 2 1/4-ounce portions and place on a cookie sheet.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until the edges start to look golden brown and crispy, 10 to 12 minutes. Do not overbake; the centers will not look fully done. Cool until cookies are set, about 30 minutes.
